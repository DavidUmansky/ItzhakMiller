Leasons in Chazanut, singing, guitar, Torah reading

Itzahak also teaching and transfering his knowledge to students. He wants that people will follow R' Shlomo's special way and will enjoy his music that combines happiness and love of Israel.

Itzhak teaches Ashkenazi Hazanut, Carlebach melodys, Torah reading, Haftara, singing, playing guitar, voice leasons. You can order private leasons and leasons for groups in class. The learning are in nice positive atmosphere, this way the students will feel comftarble and could implement what they learned. After the leasons the students will be able to perform in front of crowd with full confedence.

Courses for groups

Leasons for students from all ages - music and singing is for everyone. 5-10 interesting and practical meetings. After them you will have good expirience and confedence.

Private leasons

The best learning for some students is private leasons. You can order one leason or few, depends on how much you need. The leasons would be given in the student's house or where would be agreed.

Educational institutions

Itzhak also come to institutions like schools, colleges, universities. Music can develop the emotions and personal abilities. Music is healthy and usefull for students - relaxing and making the soul free.
