Itzhak Miller - Hasidic music singer and Carlebach cantor

Itzhak Miller is a cantor, Hasidic Music singer, composer. Additionally teaching music (vocal and instrumental), Chazanut, Torah Reading. Was student of Rabi Shlomo Carlebach and one of his closest friends. They performed together across Israel and USA and founded What is called today "Carlebach Minyans". The lyrical voice of Itzhak Miller express pure prayer and great joy and is imbued with an authentic Jewish Feeling and rythm.

Itzhak perform in different kinds of events: birthdays, Bar Mitzva's, weddings, social events, events in educational institutions, shows in religious holidays, musical Shabat, small private events, big public events. His music concerts known as a spiritual musical joy. Itzhak perform in solo-single shows with his guitar or accompanied by additional musicians.

Additionally to music concerts Itzhak also teaching Chazanut, singing, Torah Reading, guitar leasons, "Nosah Tfila" - how to lead the prayer as a cantor. You can order course, privet leasons or leasons in classes for students in various institutions.
