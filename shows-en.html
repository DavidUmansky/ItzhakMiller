Concerts

Itzhak Miller is an experienced singer, thousands of people enjoyed his music and he can fit himself to any kind of event he will perform. He make good and joyfull atmosphere in his concerts that bring a lot of joy, unite the people of Israel and connect them to G-od.

Institutions

Concert in school or high-school would be a great musical experience for the students and will pass them good educational values. Itzhak can also perform in universities/colleges.

Social event

Musical show at social event can create good vibes and atmosphere. Itzhak perform familiar songs that anyone likes. You can ask the songs in advance.

Small event

Birthday, Bar-Mitzvah, every kind of privet event you would want to organize in your house or anywhere else. Itzhak's concert will turn the event to something special and unforgettable.

Event in Synagogue or Yeshiva

Siyum Masehet, Hannuca event, Simchat-Beyt-Hashoava, Hakafot-Shniyot and more. Musical show with Hasidic songs will make the event something joyfull and energetic.

Big event

Wedding, conference, companies events. Itzhak perfored in big events behalf the municipality. His concerts achieved exposuere by thousands of viewers.

Holiday

Itzhak performs at holidays events such as Chazanut Shabat, organized holidays for families. Hasidic music and Carlebach songs can bring good and fun atmosphere that will make the soul free and relaxed.

Cazanut and melodys in Carlebach style accompanyed by musicians.
